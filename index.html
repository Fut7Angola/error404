About the brand

What is ERROR 404 LABEL?

ERROR 404 LABEL turns the digital concept of “404 Not Found” into physical fashion: rare pieces, hard to find, created for those who value exclusivity, quality and identity.

Why 404?

404 is not an error. It’s identity.

The “404 Not Found” code represents something that wasn’t found — rare, lost, outside the system. That’s exactly what we bring into clothing: pieces that don’t show up everywhere, that don’t follow the pattern, that exist in limited numbers.

“If you found this, you’re one of the few.”

Manifesto

We don’t follow trends. We create rarity.

ERROR 404 LABEL was born from the idea that what’s hard to find has value. The world is full of noise and copies — we chose the opposite: 404 units. Zero reissues.

Every piece is numbered. Every detail is intentional. We don’t make clothes for everyone — we make them for those who recognize what’s special.
