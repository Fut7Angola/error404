Description

The piece that defines the identity of ERROR 404 LABEL

An oversized unisex t‑shirt made from premium 200–220 g/m² cotton, designed to last and maintain its structure over time.

The front print, created using high‑relief screen printing (1–2 mm), adds depth, texture and visual presence. On the back, the minimalist logo appears alongside the individual piece number.

Limited to 404 units, each t‑shirt includes two numbered labels and a numbered back print, making every piece truly unique.
